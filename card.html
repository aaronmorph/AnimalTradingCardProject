Kestrel
When hunting, the Kestrel will hover before suddenly swooping down to attack its prey!
  • Latin: Falco Tinnunculus
  • Wingspan: 76 cm
  • Weight: Males 190g; Females 220g
  • Flight Speed: 39mph
Kestrels hunt during the day and around dusk. Kestrels mainly feed on small mammals and songbirds, but they will also eat beetles, insects and worms.
